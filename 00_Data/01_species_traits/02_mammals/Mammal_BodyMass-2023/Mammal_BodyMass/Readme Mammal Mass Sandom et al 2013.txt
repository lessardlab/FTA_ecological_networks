Mammal body mass data from Sandom et al. 2013 

Reference:
Sandom, C., Dalby, L., Fløjgaard, C., Kissling, W.D., Lenoir, J., Sandel, B., Trøjelsgaard, K., Ejrnæs, R. & Svenning, J.-C. (2013) Mammal predator and prey species richness are strongly linked at macroscales. Ecology, 94, 1112-1122.

Short summary
5364 mammal species total, 3946 had already data (mostly from Smith et al., 
but also some filled in by Sandom, e.g. large mammals)

1418 had no mass data, for those:

1303 mass data filled by Genus mean, and

115 with -9999 in column "Mass" (no mass data available)

FillMass
whether data have been filled (1) or not (0)

NOTE: -9999 means data are not available
